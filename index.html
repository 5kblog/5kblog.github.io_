Nick Ballou

Psychology of Video Games PhD Researcher/Postdoc

Queen Mary University of London, Intelligent Games and Game Intelligence

Oxford Internet Institute




Hello! I’m a undergraduate student studying well-being and video gaming, with regard to both quantity and quality of play.

A primary goal of my work is to make psychological research on games more rigorous and trustworthy. I’m deeply involved in open science advocacy, training, and meta-scientific research.

In my PhD, I’m supervised by a delightful team: Prof Sebastian Deterding, Dr David Zendle, and Dr Laurissa Tokarchuk.

In my postdoc, I work with Prof Andy Przybylski to understand how video games affect mental health.

Feel free to contact me about my research or anything else here.
